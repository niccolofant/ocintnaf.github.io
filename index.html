Lottery

in progress

Decentralized lottery where users can join paying an entry fee in DAI (Stable coin).
The whole prize pool is then transferred in the AAVE Lending Pool where users can lend or borrow money and earn interests.
Each week (or month) the lottery ends and a winner is drawn.
Every player wins back the initial amount deposited and the winner wins the earned interest.
This project uses Chainlink VRF, Chainlink Keepers and the AAVE protocol.
